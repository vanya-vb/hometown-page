Visit Velingrad

A town and balneological resort located in the Rhodope Mountains. It has a rich history dating back to Thracian, Roman, Slavic and Ottoman times

Top three places to visit in Velingrad

Kleptuza Spring

Kleptuza is the biggest Karst spring in Bulgaria. The park with its two lakes is one of the symbols of the town.

Tsepina Fortress

Tsepina was a castle and town. The medieval fortress has fragments of Thracian pottery and implements of labour.

Lepenitsa Cave

Lepenitsa is among the 100 Tourist Sites of Bulgaria of the Bulgarian Tourist Union.
